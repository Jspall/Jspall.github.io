Technology Report

iPhone X

The iPhone X is a new phone launched by apple with a completely new design as this iPhone is the only one to have a full screen and no button and has round corners. The iPhone X was released on November 3, 2017. This phone has the most durable glass in the smartphone, front and back. The phone has many features and allows stuff that couldn't be done in other iPhones.

Features

The iPhone X has many new and cool features that the other iPhones did not have. Some features include great camera quality, great screen quality and many other cool features:

  • The iPhone X has face recognition allowing it to be unlocked by a specific person's face only.
  • This phone also has voice recognition allowing it to sense who's using the phone and who can unlock the phone just by using their voice.
  • If the phone user doesn't have a password of any kind on the phone then it only requires a single swipe to unlock the phone.
  • The face ID can be used not only as a password but also can be used with apple pay.
  • The iPhone X has a 12MP double camera on the back including 12MP sensors, deeper pixels and new color filters.
  • The iPhone X also has dual-rear cameras that have optical image stabilization and fast lenses for outstanding photos and videos even in low light.
  • The iPhone X also introduces the A11 Bionic. The most powerful and smartest chip ever in a smartphone, with a neutral engine capable of doing up to 600 Billion operations per second.
  • iPhone X doesn't need wires as it has a wireless charger as well. It can charge with wireless charging stations and mats in hotels, cafes and airports around the world.
